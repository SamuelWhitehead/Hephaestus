ABOUT US

Why ICE was Formed?

The Innovation Club for Entrepreneurs is a stepping stone for students who want to begin or continue learning about entrepreneurship prior to and after participating in business plan competitions or starting their own businesses. We are building a community of like-minded individuals who are driven to collaborate and start their own business eventually.

Why Entrepreneurial Education Matters

Entrepreneurs learn to think creatively, manage people/resources, and communicate with a wide variety of individuals. These skills are also vital to any type of career in any field a person chooses. The ability to communicate with others is especially important for engineers and scientists, who spend 65-75% of their time working with marketing teams and giving presentations to their bosses.

Our Mission

Our vision is to foster creativity, ingenuity, and synergy in a supportive environment, to promote multidisciplinary interaction with an emphasis in business; to develop well-rounded individuals, to network with professionals, and to make a difference in the surrounding area.

Popular Events

Innovation Days

These meetings are to help members go through different processes of creating ideas, validating products with community members, and creating business plans.

Public Speakers

Entrepreneurs and professionals working in business and marketing fields are invited to share their knowledge with our members. So far we have brought in over 20 speakers ranging from local entrepreneurs to international entrepreneurs worth 300 million dollars.

Pitch Days

These days are dedicated to improving the pitching skills of our students. Students may come prepared with business pitches or be given a topic with two minutes of preparation prior to presenting.

Startup Weekend

On a Friday night, you will eat and network with 50 other competitors followed by 30-40 one minute business pitches from any member in the crowd. Ten teams will form around and spend the next 54 hours building, validating, and executing a business model prior to a pitch to investors.
